FAQ

  • How should I pay?

    You can pay after you have received the item. Payment methods such as cash, bank transfer or PAYLAH.

  • Do you charge a fee on delivery?

    YSurprisingly, we don’t, only if you follow our Instagram page :).

  • Do you charge any fee?

    No, we do not. If the seller is selling his item at $20, you can get it exactly at $20.

  • How long it takes to receive my item?

    You can decide on when to receive your goods, but it depends on the availability of our team members.

  • Can I refund the item?

    We are not responsible for any damage caused by the sellers or buyers. However, you can contact the seller directly.

  • Where can I receive my item?

    Anywhere in the NTU campus.

  • How much is the transaction fee?

    We do not charge any fee on transaction and delivery. However, we will not stop you from paying it.

  • How do you deliver my item?

    Once the deal is made, we will collect your item in any location in the NTU campus between 9am to 8pm and transfer it to the buyer. After we collect the amount from the buyer, we will fully return it back to you in due time. Of course, you may opt to collect the amount yourself.

  • What can I sell?

    You can sell new items, second-hand items or hand-made items on the platform. Only items that are legally approve by the law of Singapore can be sold. Bubble gum and any kind of cigarette is strictly not allowed. We retain the right to delist your item if we discover that the item is not promoting social goods.

  • Can I sell refrigerator?

    You can sell refrigerator. However, we do not provide delivery service on any item which is greater than 5kg. You may consider to deliver yourself.

  • I am not a NTU student, can I sell on your platform?

    Uh… also can.

  • Anything can be delivered?

    We will not process any delivery order if it is greater than 5kg. Our team retain the right to decide whether or not to delivery the item.

  • How much should I pay for the delivery?

    We will provide free delivery to all the university students if the item is below 5kg. We will not process any delivery order if it is greater than 5kg.

  • Do you charge a fee on delivery?

    Surprisingly, we don’t, only if you follow our Instagram page :).
